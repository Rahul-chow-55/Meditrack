Razorpay is India's most trusted payment infrastructure, used by 10 million+ businesses. We chose it for MediTrack because it offers native UPI support, robust webhook reliability, instant refund APIs, and compliance with RBI guidelines — ensuring every patient payment is safe, fast, and reversible.
